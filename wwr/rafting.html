Why Explore with us?

Adrenaline rush: The thrill of the rapids, the sound of rushing water, and the fear of the unknown create a rush of adrenaline that can be addictive.

Scenery and natural beauty: White water rafting often takes place in stunning natural environments, such as rivers, canyons, and forests, providing a unique perspective and appreciation of nature.

Challenge: The unpredictable nature of white water rafting presents a challenge to the participants, both physically and mentally. It requires teamwork, communication, and quick decision-making skills to navigate the rapids safely.

Social interaction: White water rafting is often done with a group of people, providing a great opportunity for social interaction and bonding.

Escape from routine: It's an escape from the mundane routine of daily life, providing a sense of adventure and excitement that many people crave.


Our Priority

The safety of our guests is our top priority and our years of worldwide experience allow us to ensure your safety at all times on the rafting tours. Paddling on white water is not only a job for us but also our passion. Our goal is to share this passion and inspire people to unplug, go out and explore this amazing playground, we call our home. We offer rafting tours for first-time rafters, adventurous beginners, and experienced paddlers. With our local knowledge, we will make sure that you can enjoy unique adventures in the beautiful Swiss Alps.
