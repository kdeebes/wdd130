Overview

Purpose

The site will be used to create an engaging and user-friendly online platform for customers to explore, and select a wide variety of high-quality rugs. Our intended audience for this site includes interior designers, homeowners, and anyone seeking premium rug options to enhance their living spaces.

Audience

The audience will be those with a discerning eye for home decor and a desire to elevate their living spaces, 'Rugs Are Us' is your ultimate destination. We offer a curated collection of exquisite rugs, meticulously crafted to enhance your interior design, providing a seamless shopping experience that caters to your unique style and preferences.

Welcome to Rugs Are Us

Welcome to Rugs Are Us, your one-stop destination for all your rug needs. Whether you're looking for stylish area rugs to add a pop of color to your living room, cozy shag rugs for your bedroom, or durable outdoor rugs to elevate your patio decor, we have a wide selection that caters to every style and budget. With our extensive collection, expert craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service, we strive to provide you with the perfect rug that will transform your space into a warm and inviting haven. Explore our website today and discover the perfect rug to enhance your home.


As you explore our website, you'll find an intuitive and user-friendly platform designed to make your rug-shopping experience seamless and enjoyable. Browse through our categories, filter your preferences, and immerse yourself in the world of textures, colors, and styles. Each rug comes with detailed descriptions and high-resolution images to help you make an informed decision. We want you to feel confident that the rug you choose will not only meet but exceed your expectations.

About Us

Rugs Are Us is a family-owned and operated business that has been serving the community for over X years. Our passion for rugs began when we were children, and we've always loved the way they can make a home feel more inviting and comfortable.

Our Mission: Our mission is to help you find the perfect rug for your home. We believe that everyone deserves to have a beautiful rug that they love, and we're here to make that happen.

Our Quality: We're committed to providing our customers with the best possible quality rugs. All of our rugs are made from high-quality materials and are built to last.

Our Selection: We offer a wide variety of rugs from all over the world, including traditional, contemporary, and designer rugs. We also have a large selection of rugs in all shapes and sizes, so you can find the perfect rug for your space.

Our Expertise: Our team of experts is always happy to help you find the perfect rug for your needs. We can help you choose the right rug for your style, budget, and space.

Our Guarantee: We're so confident in the quality of our rugs that we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your rug, you can return it for a full refund.
